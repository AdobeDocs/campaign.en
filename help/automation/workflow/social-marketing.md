# Social Marketing {#social-marketing}

The workflows detailed below are installed with the **Social Marketing** module by default. This module enables integration with X (formerly known as Twitter).


>[!AVAILABILITY]
>
>`:warning:` Social Marketing with Facebook is only available in Campaign Classic v7.  

<table> 
 <tbody> 
  <tr> 
   <td> <strong>Label</strong><br /> </td> 
   <td> <strong>Internal name</strong><br /> </td> 
   <td> <strong>Description</strong><br /> </td> 
  </tr> 
  <tr> 
   <td> <span class="uicontrol">Calculation of Twitter statistics</span> <br /> </td> 
   <td> <span class="uicontrol">statsTwitter</span> <br /> </td> 
   <td> This workflow calculates statistics linked to retweets and visits on X (formerly known as Twitter).<br /> </td> 
  </tr> 
  <tr> 
   <td> <span class="uicontrol">Synchronizing Twitter pages</span> <br /> </td> 
   <td> <span class="uicontrol">syncTwitter</span> <br /> </td> 
   <td> This workflow imports X followers into Adobe Campaign every day at 7am.<br /> </td> 
  </tr> 
  <tr> 
   <td> <span class="uicontrol">Calculation of Facebook statistics (v7 only)</span> <br /> </td> 
   <td> <span class="uicontrol">statsFacebook</span> <br /> </td> 
   <td> This workflow calculates statistics linked to interactions with Facebook fans.<br /> </td> 
  </tr> 
  <tr> 
   <td> <span class="uicontrol">Synchronizing Facebook fans (v7 only)</span> <br /> </td> 
   <td> <span class="uicontrol">syncFacebookFans</span> <br /> </td> 
   <td> This workflow imports Facebook fans into Adobe Campaign every day at 7am.<br /> </td> 
  </tr> 
  <tr> 
   <td> <span class="uicontrol">Synchronizing Facebook pages (v7 only)</span> <br /> </td> 
   <td> <span class="uicontrol">syncFacebook</span> <br /> </td> 
   <td> This workflow synchronizes Facebook pages with Adobe Campaign every day at 7am.<br /> </td> 
  </tr> 
 </tbody> 
</table>
